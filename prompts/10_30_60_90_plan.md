# 30-60-90 Day Plan Prompt

You are an expert career coach. Create a realistic 30-60-90 day plan for the new role.

## Input
- Job requirements (JSON): {{job_json}}
- Enhanced resume (JSON): {{resume_json}}
- Company: {{company}}
- Role: {{role}}
- Language: {{language}}

## Task
Create a 30-60-90 day plan that demonstrates understanding of the role and proactive planning.

## Output Structure

```markdown
# 30-60-90 Day Plan
## {{role}} at {{company}}

### Overview
This plan outlines my approach to ramping up effectively and delivering value in my first 90 days.

---

## Days 1-30: Learn & Absorb

### Goals
- Understand team dynamics and processes
- Learn internal tools and systems
- Build relationships with key stakeholders
- Complete onboarding requirements

### Key Actions

#### Week 1: Orientation
- [ ] Complete HR onboarding and compliance training
- [ ] Set up development environment and tools
- [ ] Meet with manager to align on expectations
- [ ] Review team documentation and wikis

#### Week 2: Deep Dive
- [ ] Shadow team members on key processes
- [ ] Understand current projects and priorities
- [ ] Map stakeholders and their needs
- [ ] Start attending regular team meetings

#### Weeks 3-4: Initial Contributions
- [ ] Take on first small task/ticket
- [ ] Ask questions and document learnings
- [ ] Identify quick wins
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with cross-functional partners

### Success Metrics
- Completed onboarding checklist
- Documented understanding of key systems
- Established relationships with team members

---

## Days 31-60: Contribute & Add Value

### Goals
- Take ownership of specific areas
- Deliver measurable contributions
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Build credibility through results

### Key Actions
- [ ] Own [specific area based on job requirements]
- [ ] Complete [type of project] independently
- [ ] Propose 1-2 process improvements
- [ ] Contribute to team knowledge base

### Success Metrics
- [Specific deliverable]
- Positive feedback from manager/peers
- Identified and documented improvements

---

## Days 61-90: Lead & Optimize

### Goals
- Demonstrate expertise in role
- Drive initiatives independently
- Mentor/help newer team members
- Contribute to strategic planning

### Key Actions
- [ ] Lead [initiative based on job requirements]
- [ ] Present results/learnings to team
- [ ] Propose and implement optimization
- [ ] Set goals for next quarter with manager

### Success Metrics
- [Measurable outcome]
- Clear path forward established
- Recognition from stakeholders

---

### Questions for My Manager
1. What does success look like in the first 90 days?
2. What are the biggest challenges the team is facing?
3. Who are the key stakeholders I should build relationships with?
4. What resources are available for learning and development?
```

## CRITICAL RULES
1. Keep the plan realistic and achievable
2. Align with actual job requirements
3. Show initiative without overstepping
4. Include measurable success metrics
5. Demonstrate understanding of typical onboarding
